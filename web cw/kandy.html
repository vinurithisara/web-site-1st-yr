Kandy

"Kandy is a major city situated in the central province of Sri Lanka. It was the last capital of the Sri Lankan age of ancient kings.
Some days the skies of Kandy seem to be constantly darkened,
with persistent mist clinging to the hills surrounding the magnificent centrepiece lake of the region".

Temple of tooth relic

The Holy Tooth's golden-roofed temple houses the most famous Buddhist relic of Sri Lanka-a Buddha's tooth. The heavily guarded room which houses the tooth is open to devotees and tourists during puja (offerings or prayers). In reality you don't see the tooth, though. It's held in a gold barrel shaped like a dagoba (stupa), which includes a series of decreasing size six dagoba caskets.

Royal Botanic Gardens

Royal Botanical Gardens at Peradeniya is Sri Lanka 's oldest formal gardens. It is situated about 5 km west of the city of Kandy in the central province of Sri Lanka, 460 meters above the mean sea level. Peradeniya Royal Botanic Gardens draws about 2 million local and international tourists annually.
