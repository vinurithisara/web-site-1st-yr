HIKKADUWA

Hikkaduwa is a town ,situated between Galle and Ambalangoda in Galle district. Beautiful beach and the coral reef provide the glamour to this amazing town. Both foriegn and local tourists who likes to spend their lesuretime with sunbathing, diving, swimming, surfing and also enjoy the beauty of the corals, come to Hikkaduwa.

With this attractive events and scenaries most of the people around hikkaduwa choose tourism as their livelihood.Therefore we can see so many hotels around this area. More over there are some music festivals like Hikkatranz in Hikkaduwa area.


Corals

Corals carry a great value for this amazing town. Hikkaduwa national park is named for reserve coral reefs. It contains very high biodiversity with some rare species of corals and fish. People are highly informed to protect this amazing environment to the future.

Activities

1.Glass bottom boat rides

One of the best thing to do in hikkaduwa is go glass bottom boat rides. There are boats covered in glass or transparent bottoms in order to give tourists the opportunity to explore the sea in the simplest possible way. There are many types of butterfly fish, as well as you can find several types of angelfish, and colorful coral reefs during your ride here.

2.Surfing

Surfing is one of the best things to do when you are in Hikkaduwa. The waves are suited to do surfing not only for experts but also for beginners. From December to March is the best time duration for surfing. So the beach at Hikkaduwa gives surfers plenty to enjoy in a good environment.

Nightlife

One of Hikkaduwa's favorite things to do is enjoy its nightlife. This lovely city gives both beachside restaurants and a vibrant nightlife and has proven to be one of the most popular destinations for travelers to enjoy an unforgettable night out. There are different discos on the shoreline where you can dance all night long and enjoy a friendly atmosphere.
